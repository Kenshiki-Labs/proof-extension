01 · The CauseIdentity Manufacturing at Industrial Scale

AI reduced the cost and time of manufacturing an identity to near zero — a face, documents, a voice, a paper trail.

The Governance Blind Spot

Nearly all AI governance aims at making models trustworthy: alignment, guardrails, scores. But the adversary runs their own models, on their own hardware, outside every guardrail we will ever ship. Certifying the speaker does nothing when the actor is never ours to govern.

The Industry’s Broken Bet

Score the applicant. Adjudicate ability to repay. Assume existence. That assumption is now the hack — identities manufactured at industrial scale by fraud rings and nation-state actors using the same tools we do.

The Inversion

Stop trying to trust a model — any model. Govern the action instead. Trust set to zero, permanently: no session records a completion unless the physics of a life, a passport chip read, and a Face ID token are cryptographically bound.

A better fake still has to clear a physical bind it cannot manufacture from any amount of inference.

05 · The MechanismBeyond the Login: Continuous Authentication

If malware steals an active session cookie, the attacker bypasses the login screen entirely. Phenotyping is the only mechanism that catches the shift in physical behavior mid-session.

1 · INITIAL LOGIN

User authenticates via standard methods (passkey / password). Baseline kinematics begin tracking.

2 · SESSION HIJACKED

Attacker injects a stolen cookie locally. No new login prompt is triggered.

3 · PHENOTYPE MISMATCH

Scroll arc, typing rhythm, and device state instantly diverge from baseline. Session revoked.

09 · The PipelineFrom Physical Interaction to Confirmation

Abstracting physical interaction into mathematical vectors decouples verification from personal data.

1 · RAW MATERIAL

The host environment: iOS, Android, browser, IAL3 document.

2 · OBSERVATION

Passive monitoring of kinematics, device state, and rhythm.

3 · TELEMETRY

Movement extracted into raw vectors (deltaY, durationMs).

4 · FUSION

Distinct vectors merged into one joint distribution. PII enters here — and stops here.

5 · CONFIRMATION

PII is collected once, at the identity legs, and never crosses the fusion boundary.

Consumers receive a score, not a person.

11 · The BuildThe V1 Trust Stack — Built, Not Proposed

Every layer of the story arc is shipped software. A relying party can ask for proof at the moment of risk, receive bounded evidence, and complete or stop the action with an auditable reason.

Show

Pulse Proof

A 4-line embed on any customer site. Demonstrates that ordinary web sessions already emit presence and continuity signals — before any SDK is integrated.

See

Pulse Lens

Browser-local observation, honest by design: who is watching the tab, what was blocked, and what remains exposed. Chrome, Edge, Firefox.

Prove

Pulse SDK

Signed, bounded device-physics evidence via the Pulse Core engine — no raw sensor streams, no stable hardware identifiers. Production SwiftPM package.

Decide

Pulse Gate

The server orchestrator and session database: bonded sessions, App Attest verification, and fail-closed completion gates. Evidence becomes an enforceable decision.

Ground

Pulse Anchor

The identity legs: CAMARA carrier binding and passport MRZ + NFC chip read, plus trust-score rails — so decisions never rest on device evidence alone.

Not passive tracking. Not ad-tech fingerprinting. Not a login replacement. A bounded assurance system.

14 · The BusinessROI & Privacy by Architecture

Higher Conversion

Invisible, continuous authentication removes high-friction MFA prompts and CAPTCHAs — eliminating drop-off at critical checkout and application flows.

Broken Ring Economics

Attacker marginal cost rises from near-zero to thousands of dollars per identity. Synthetic identity rings cannot script a phenotype — they are forced into expensive, manual human labor that destroys their ROI.

PII Behind the Boundary

Identity documents are read once and sealed at the fusion boundary. Downstream systems consume a confirmation score — no personal identifiers to store, breach, or subpoena.
